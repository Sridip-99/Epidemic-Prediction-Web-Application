About Us

Welcome to our project on Prediction by Machine Learning. We are a team of four final-year students working under the guidance of Professor Dr. Biswajit Sir to create a predictive model aimed at understanding and anticipating epidemic growth. Using a Random Forest Classifier and a Random Forest Regressor, our model analyzes various parameters such as: Location, Time, Population Density, Case Growth Rate, Death Growth Rate, Death to Case Ratio to provide probability-based insights on epidemic trends. This tool is designed to help public health agencies and communities better prepare for potential outbreaks by analyzing and visualizing critical data.
